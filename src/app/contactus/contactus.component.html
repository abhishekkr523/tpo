The Engineering College Jhalawar, Rajasthan was established in 2007 in the Jhalawar district of Rajasthan, India.
The Engineering College Jhalawar is affiliated to the Rajasthan Technical University Kota and the courses are duly approved by AICTE New-Delhi.[citation needed]
The college is spread in more than 45 acres. There is facilities of sports and library.

Road Transport

Almost all the towns and most of the important places are well connected with the district headquarter. The Jaipur-Bhopal National Highway(NH-12) also passes through Jhalawar & jhalrapatan and college is situated .

Rail Transport

The district is connected with rail facilities at three places viz. Bhawani Mandi ,Ramganj Mandi and kota is the main juntion which comes in the way of Delhi-Mumbai broad gauge line of Western Railway.

Air Transport

The nearby international airport are at Jaipur (330 kms), Bhopal (218 Kms) and Indore(227Kms)
