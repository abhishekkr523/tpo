Office of Career services

The Placement Department at our engineering college is a dynamic and dedicated hub that plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of our students. Committed to bridging the gap between academia and industry, our placement team tirelessly works to prepare students for successful careers by providing them with the necessary skills and opportunities. Through a combination of rigorous training, industry-focused workshops, and personalized career counseling, the department equips students with the knowledge and confidence they need to excel in the competitive job market. With a strong network of industry connections, our placement cell facilitates internships and job placements, ensuring that our graduates step into the professional world well-prepared and with a competitive edge. The Placement Department is not just a department but a gateway to a world of opportunities, where dreams are realized, and futures are shaped.
