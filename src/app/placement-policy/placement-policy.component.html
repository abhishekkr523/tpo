Placement Policy


    The Office of Training & Placement have following placement policies:
  1. Periodic Conduction of IQ tests.
  2. Arrangement of Personality Development Workshops.
  3. Trains students in aptitude tests, interview techniques, group discussions.
  4. Notification regarding various competitive examinations.
  5. Organizes industrial visits.
  6. Guides students wishing to pursue Higher Education.
